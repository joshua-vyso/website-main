# Vyso — Design Brief

## Company

**Vyso** (vyso.co.za) is an AI-powered operations and automation company for food businesses in Africa. We diagnose operational chaos, automate the repetitive work, and build lightweight custom apps so owners can stop running their business on WhatsApp and spreadsheets.

**One sentence:** "We build automation systems and simple custom apps for food businesses — so owners spend less time managing chaos and more time running their business."

**What Vyso is NOT:** Not a generic software agency. Not enterprise software. Not an accountant.

---

## Brand

**Tone:** Direct. Confident. Clean. No startup fluff, no jargon. Speaks to a food business owner who is exhausted, not a tech buyer.

**Aesthetic:** Light, sleek, minimal. Think Linear or Vercel — precision-engineered — but warmer and grounded in an African context. Lots of white space. Typography-led. Subtle, intentional interactions.

**Typography:** Barlow Condensed — weights 400, 500, 700. Headings are large and direct. Body is readable and airy.

**Colours:**
- Background: `#FFFFFF` (white)
- Primary text: `#0A0A0A` (near black)
- Accent: `#3375AE` (blue — used sparingly: buttons, borders, highlights)
- Subtle surface: `#F5F5F5` (light grey for card backgrounds)
- Muted text: `#6B6B6B`

**Logo:** "VYSO" wordmark — V Y S and a filled circle as the O. Black on white. Already animated as an intro sequence on the homepage.

## Design System Notes

- **Spacing:** Generous. 80–120px between sections. Breathing room is the design.
- **Borders:** 1px, `#E5E5E5` — used for card edges, header underline, tier separators.
- **Buttons:** Filled (blue `#3375AE`, white text) for primary CTAs. Outlined (1px blue border, blue text) for secondary.
- **Cards:** White background, 1px `#E5E5E5` border, subtle shadow on hover. No rounded corners (sharp = precise).
- **Animations:** Staggered fade-in on scroll for sections. Nothing flashy. Precision over drama.
- **Mobile:** Mobile-first. Single column on small screens. Bento grid collapses to 2-col then 1-col.
